The majority of my free time is spent biking. From [[gravel-biking|gravel]] to road to [[mountain-biking|mountain]], give me two rubber wheels and I'm happy. 

Currently, I am the off-road captain of the [Husky Cycling Team](https://www.huskycyclinguw.com/). If you're around UW and looking for a group ride, feel free to join! Saturdays at 10 am (usually).

Every ride is tracked on my [Strava](https://www.strava.com/athletes/81998129). Careful, I might take your KOMs.
